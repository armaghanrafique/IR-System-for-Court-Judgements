ORDER. 

This petition has arisen out of the impugned order dated 01.03.2016 passed by the learned Chief Court whereby the CFA No. 03/2012 filed by the petitioner was dismissed holding it meritless by maintaining the order dated 24.04.2009 of the learned Senior Civil Judge Skardu., hence, this petition for leave to appeal. 

This court vide order dated 24.11.2016 issued notice to the respondent and the case is heard today. 2. 

The learned counsel for the petitioner submits that the respondent filed a Civil Suit No. 17/2008 in the court of learned Senior Civil Judge Skardu for recovery of Rs.1 0 millions as damages for defamation.
