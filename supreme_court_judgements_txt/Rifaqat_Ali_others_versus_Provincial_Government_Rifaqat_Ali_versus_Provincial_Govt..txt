ORDER. 

This petition for leave to appeal was directed against the impugned order dated 17.08.2015 in Civil Misc. 

No. 296/2014 passed by the learned Gilgit -Baltistan Chief Court. 

Whereby the said Civil Misc was dismissed being meritless. 

The petitioners being aggrieved by and dissatisfied with filed this petition for leave to appeal. 

This court vide order dated 07.04.2016 issued notices to the respondents and the case was fixed for final arguments today on 28.06.2016.
