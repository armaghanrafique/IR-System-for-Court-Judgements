court cannot comment on merits at this state and since deceased di ed in police custody, therefore, notwithstanding the ultimate result of the case, the police may settle the matter with legal heirs of deceased to their satisfaction and instead of seeking any favourable order from the court may enter into compromise with them by paying compensation, as is permissible under the law, before the learned sessions in view of the position explained by the advocate general and deputy secretary home, no further order is required to be passed and there is also no nee to keep this case pending,
