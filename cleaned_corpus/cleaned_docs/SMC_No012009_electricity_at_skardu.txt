(electricity at skardu) chief judge. advocate general for gilgit-baltistan advocate s.e, pwd division skardu xen water power division skardu deputy secretary water power gilgit-baltistan -- the deputy secretary water power assisted by exn skardu states that total consumption of electricity in skardu city is mega watt, whereas mw electricity is already in production and shortage of mw is not material to create electricity problem in city. he further states that a power project with the aid of asian development bank will be constructed (kachura) of the capacity of mw and project will be started on release of fund by adb. advocate has stated that presently there is no shortage of electricity in city. in view of the position explained by the deputy secretary water and power assisted by xen division skardu and the deputy secretary has stated that in case of shortage of electricity the alternate arrangement of diesel generators already available with the department in skardu, subject to availability of fund can be operated. in view thereof, further proceeding into the matter are closed and case is disposed of accordingly. chief
